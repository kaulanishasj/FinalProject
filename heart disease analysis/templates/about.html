Heart Disease Prevalance

Heart disease is the leading cause of death in America, with 610,000 people dying every year according to CDC By spreading awareness about the prevalance and causes of heart diseases, we hope to encourage everybody to take proper care of their cardiovascular system

Heart Disease Prediction

Using our interactive application, people can input their personal data such as age, sex, cholestral levels, etc. we plan to provide a personalized assessment of their chances of suffering from heart disease. We also hope to suggest to them changes they should incorporate in their lifestyle to take better game of themselves.
